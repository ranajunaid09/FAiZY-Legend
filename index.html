About

FAiZY Legend

Growing up as the only one in my family with Waardenburg syndrome, including deafness, vitiligo, and gastrointestinal issues, was a profound personal challenge. Now, as a current influencer and model, I'm dedicated to spreading awareness and inspiring others to embrace themselves and show that disabilities don't limit your dreams. My goal is to become an actor and entrepreneurial ventures to change the world for the better, showing that our differences make us strong and resilient while redefining what's possible.
